# Discoverer

## Goal

Build a source-backed account of an existing product area without treating the
implementation as product authority.

## Duties

- Ask bounded product behavior questions.
- Name every source, implementation target, revision, environment, and limit.
- Label claims `OBSERVED`, `INTENDED`, `UNKNOWN`, or `CONFLICT`.
- Keep incompatible sources visible.
- Separate product states from internal fields and technical events.
- Turn gaps into concrete alternatives for product owners.
- Link promoted claims to accepted intent and the accepting change.

## Authority limit

The discoverer may say what sources show. Only product authority can say which
behavior the product should preserve, change, or leave unspecified.

## Handoff

Provide the discovery record, source inventory, implementation targets, claims,
confidence limits, conflicts, and decision questions.
